Ph.D. Student: Our group is recruiting new PhD students every intake batch. For more details, please refer to the SMU SCIS Ph.D. online application. Visiting Research Students/Scholars: Our group welcomes self-funded (CSC-funded/School-funded) visiting research students/scholors. Our group also has several SMU-funded visting student positions avaiable (standard monthly stipend of 2700 SGD and at least 1-year visiting duration).

Remote Collaboration: Collaboration duration is at least 6 months for completing one research paper.

Application: Please email your CV and transcripts with a short self-introduction to Dr. DENG (ydeng at smu dot edu dot sg) with the subject "PhD/Visiting/Remote Application - Your Name - Your Institution". Reply on a bi-weekly basis if match.
