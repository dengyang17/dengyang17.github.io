I am currently a tenure-track Assistant Professor in School of Computing and Information Systems, Singapore Management University. Prior to that, I was a postdoctoral research fellow in NExT++, National University of Singapore, working with Prof. Tat-Seng Chua and Prof. See-Kiong Ng. I received my Ph.D degree at The Chinese University of Hong Kong, supervised by Prof. Wai Lam (CUHK Text Mining Group).

My research mainly lies in Natural Language Processing (NLP), Information Retrieval (IR), and Large Language Models (LLM) – with special focus on

  • Proactive Conversational AI
    Survey [IJCAI 2023], Tutorial [ACL 2023] [SIGIR-AP 2023],
    Non-collaborative Dialogues [ICLR 2024] [EMNLP 2024] [EMNLP 2023 Findings],
    Target-guided Dialogues [EMNLP 2024] [EMNLP 2024 Findings] [TOIS 2023],
    Emotional Support Dialogues [ACL 2024] [ACL 2023],
    Clarifications [ACL 2024 Findings] [EMNLP 2022]
  • Trust and Reliability of Large Language Models
    Knowledge Boundary [Survey] [EMNLP 2024] [EMNLP 2024] [ACL 2024],
    Robustness [ICLR 2025] [AAAI 2025],
    Hallucination [EMNLP 2024 Findings] [EMNLP 2023],
    Safety [arxiv] [EMNLP 2023 Findings]
  • Language Model Powered Agents
    Tutorial [WWW 2024] [SIGIR 2024], Role-playing Agent [Survey],
    Human-Agent Interaction [ACL 2024] [EMNLP 2024 Findings], Memory [NAACL 2025]
  • Human-centered Information Seeking
