I am currently a tenure-track Assistant Professor in School of Computing and Information Systems, Singapore Management University (SMU). Prior to that, I was a postdoctoral research fellow in NExT++, National University of Singapore, working with Prof. Tat-Seng Chua and Prof. See-Kiong Ng. I received my Ph.D degree at The Chinese University of Hong Kong, supervised by Prof. Wai Lam.

I am leading the CHAT NLP Group where we conduct research on Natural Language Processing (NLP), Information Retrieval (IR), and Large Language Models (LLM) – with special focus on
